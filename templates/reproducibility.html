Reproducibility Calculator

Instructions for use

  1. Select the sample type and temperature that most closely matches the results you are checking the reproducibility of from the drop-down menu
  2. If you are unsure what sample type to select, click here to open the guidance page
  3. Enter the two viscosity results into the labelled boxes
  4. Click the calculate button and the tool will perform the calculations and tell you whether the results are repeatable or not
  5. Click the reset button to clear the inputs and outputs and ready the tool to check more results
