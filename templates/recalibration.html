Viscometer Recalibration

Instructions for use

  1. Determine acceleration due to gravity at your laboratory's location
  2. Determine the acceleration due to gravity at the standardization laboratory's location
  3. Click here to open the guidance page for more information on viscometer recalibration
  4. Input the constant of the viscometer you wish to recalibrate
  5. Input these two values into the labelled boxes
  6. Press calculateand the tool with provide the percentage difference
  7. If the percentage difference is greater than 0.1%, the tool will then provide a new constant for the viscometer
  8. Click the reset button to clear the inputs and outputs and ready the tool to recalibrate another viscometer
